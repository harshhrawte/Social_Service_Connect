Volunteer Programs

Location: Various neighborhoods in the city

Date: Every first Saturday of the month

Description: Join Clean Earth NGO in their mission to keep our communities clean and green. Volunteer for a morning of litter clean-up, graffiti removal, and beautification projects in local parks, streets, and public spaces. Make a tangible difference in the environment while connecting with like-minded individuals in your community.

Location: Downtown area and shelters

Date: Every Wednesday evening

Description: Partner with Shelter Care Organization to provide support and resources to individuals experiencing homelessness. Volunteer for street outreach teams, distributing essential supplies such as food, water, hygiene kits, and blankets.

Location: Local food bank warehouse

Date: Every second Saturday of the month

Description: Organize monthly food drives to help stock the shelves of our food bank. Responsibilities include coordinating volunteers, managing food collection, and overseeing the distribution of collected items.

Location: Senior living facilities and community centers

Date: Every Thursday morning

Description: Support the well-being of elderly individuals by participating in the Senior Wellness Program. Volunteer for fitness classes, recreational activities, and social events designed to promote physical health and mental stimulation among seniors. Share your time and companionship to bring joy and vitality to older adults in our community.

Location: Local high schools and youth centers

Date: Quarterly workshops (dates vary)

Description: Inspire and empower the next generation by volunteering with Future Leaders NGO. Participate in interactive workshops and mentoring sessions focused on leadership development, career exploration, and personal growth for high school students. Volunteer mentors provide guidance, encouragement, and real-world insights to help youth unlock their potential and achieve their goals.
